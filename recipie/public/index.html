Recipes 1 - 5 Bean Chili Stew Dish

1. In a large pot, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add diced onions and sauté until translucent.

2. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.

3. Stir in a can of diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, and a blend of five different beans (such as kidney beans, black beans, pinto beans, cannellini beans, and chickpeas).

4. Season with chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper to taste. You can also add a pinch of red pepper flakes for some heat.

5. Simmer the stew for 20-30 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Serve hot and garnish with fresh cilantro if desired.

Recipes 2 - Veg Noodles Dish

1, Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

2, Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large pan over medium heat.

3 ,Add minced garlic and sliced onion. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent.

4 ,Add julienned carrots, sliced bell pepper, cabbage, and green beans. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender yet still crisp.

5 ,In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, vinegar, tomato ketchup, salt, black pepper powder, and red chili flakes (if using).

6 ,Add the cooked noodles to the pan and pour the sauce mixture over the noodles and vegetables.

7 ,Toss everything together gently, ensuring that the noodles and vegetables are coated with the sauce evenly.

8 ,Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring continuously, until the noodles are heated through.

9 ,Remove from heat and garnish with chopped spring onions.

10 ,Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Veg Noodles!

Recipes 3 - Tofu Curry

1 ,Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or skillet over medium heat.

2 ,Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Sauté until the onion becomes translucent and the mixture becomes fragrant.

3 ,Add the cubed tofu to the pan and cook for a few minutes until the tofu is lightly browned.

4 ,Add the sliced bell pepper, carrot, and zucchini to the pan. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.

5 ,In a small bowl, mix together curry powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt.

6 ,Sprinkle the spice mixture over the tofu and vegetables in the pan. Stir well to coat everything evenly.

7 ,Pour in the coconut milk and stir gently to combine all the ingredients.

8 ,Reduce the heat to low and simmer the curry for about 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together and the vegetables to cook through.

9 ,Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.

10 ,Remove from heat and garnish with freshly chopped cilantro.

11 ,Serve the tofu curry hot with cooked rice or naan bread.
